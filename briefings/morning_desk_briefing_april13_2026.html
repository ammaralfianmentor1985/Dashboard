BREAKING: US Navy blockade of Strait of Hormuz begins today at 10:00 AM ET. WTI +8% overnight to ~$104. Dow, S&P and Nasdaq futures all down 1%+.
This is the exact reversal we warned about on April 9. Four days ago, markets partied on ceasefire euphoria. Today the bill arrives. That distinction matters for the oil supply calculation — it's less severe than a full Hormuz closure, but the market doesn't trust the execution yet.
