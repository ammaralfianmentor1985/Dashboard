⚠ Critical Warning — Sharp Sell-Off & MSCI Uncertainty Looms

The IHSG plunged -1.29% to 6,037.52 in the first session of 23 June, with 437 stocks declining and the financial sector down 3.03% — driven by a mix of Middle East geopolitical tension and market anxiety ahead of the final MSCI Market Classification Review verdict (expected around 24 June WIB). ⚑ Watch Note — Portfolio Outflows & Subsidy-Driven Inflation Risk
