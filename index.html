⚠ High Risk · High Reward

Not recommended for new traders. Huge liquidity but extremely volatile — it is very easy to get a Margin Call. Only risk what you can afford to lose entirely.

✓ Low Risk · High Return Investment — US & Global Indices

Stocks are best for short to long-term investing.

⚠ High Risk · Low Return — Forex Market Best for $250

Forex is one of the highest-risk markets. With small capital the recommended volume is 0.03–0.05 lots with a maximum profit target of ~$15 per trade. Proceed with extreme caution.

★ Best for Active Trading — Commodities

Gold: keep at least 50% of your portfolio in gold as a store of value. US Oil (WTI): a minimum trade of ~10 barrels is recommended; each barrel moves in line with the live WTI price shown above.

✓ Moderate Risk — Major Market Indices

Indices track entire markets (S&P 500, Nasdaq, Dow, DAX, Nikkei). Best held long-term through index funds or ETFs rather than traded short-term — a core, lower-volatility building block for most portfolios.
